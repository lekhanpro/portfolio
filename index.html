FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about my services and working methods.

What types of projects do you work on?

Mainly: B2B/B2C SaaS, web platforms, content sites with technical SEO, and Stripe integrations. I am less adapted for native mobile applications.

Do you work remotely or on-site?

Both: Remote by default (worldwide), or on-site depending on the location and project needs.

Are you available for long missions?

Yes. I work on missions of 1 week (audit), 1 to 4 weeks (feature development), or 1 to 6 months (full product). Availability to be confirmed based on current planning.

Do you offer maintenance after delivery?

Yes. Punctual support or monthly maintenance contracts for continuous evolutions. Defined at the end of the project.

Do you do Design / UX?

I am a developer, not a designer. If you like the style of this portfolio, that's exactly what I can deliver. I can integrate Figma mockups faithfully or design functional, sober interfaces with Tailwind. I do not offer full branding or UX workshops.
